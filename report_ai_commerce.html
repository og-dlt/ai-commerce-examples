AI Commerce Delegation Landscape

AI Commerce is evolving from recommendation interfaces into execution-capable agents. The strategic signal is delegation maturity: who does the work in each stage of the consumer journey.

1. Advises and compares

AI reduces search and evaluation burden while consumers still execute actions.

2. Acts with permission

AI starts executing steps after explicit approval checkpoints.

3. Completes transactions

AI drives checkout or booking completion in transaction-ready flows.

4. Manages purchases over time

AI continuously optimizes recurring spend and post-purchase outcomes.
